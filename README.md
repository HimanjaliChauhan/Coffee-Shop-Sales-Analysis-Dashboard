☕ Coffee Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee shop sales data to uncover revenue trends, product performance, and customer purchasing behavior. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights using Power BI.

📊 Dashboard Highlights
$82K+ Total Sales analyzed
17K+ Orders processed
Revenue breakdown by product category
Top 10 best-selling products
Hourly and weekday sales heatmap
Custom interactive tooltips for drill-down insights

🔍 Key Insights

Coffee contributes the highest share of total revenue
A small group of products drives a large portion of sales
Peak sales occur during specific weekday hours
